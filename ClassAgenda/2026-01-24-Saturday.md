### Exam Day Reminders
- **Duration**: 120 minutes
- **Question types**: Multiple choice, case studies, drag-and-drop, hotspot
- **Passing score**: 700/1000
- **Cost**: ~$165 USD (check current pricing)
- **Format**: Online proctored or testing center
- **Register**: [Microsoft Certification Dashboard](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/certifications/)

### Last-Minute Tips
1. **Get sleep**: Rest is more valuable than cramming the night before
2. **Read carefully**: Watch for keywords (NOT, EXCEPT, LEAST, MOST)
3. **Time management**: ~2 minutes per question, flag difficult ones
4. **Trust yourself**: Your preparation has been thorough
5. **Schedule soon**: Take the exam within 1 month while knowledge is fresh

## Optional Group Activity: Custom Domain & Identity
- **Objective**: Configure a custom domain and test identity integration.
- **Steps**:
  1. Create an **Azure DNS Zone** for your custom domain.
  2. Verify the domain in **Microsoft Entra ID**.
  3. Create a new user: `admin@yourdomain.com`.
  4. Test login with the new user identity.
